Education

Education

Calculus, linear algebra, physics, logic, and space science. The emphasis is on treating mathematics not as a list of formulas to memorize, but as a language for describing the world.

Teaching

Courses and Teaching Focus

Miyamoto teaches foundational mathematics and physics, mainly at Akita Prefectural University, with additional part-time lectures at other institutions.

Calculus

Calculus Ia/IIa and related first-year calculus courses, with attention to applications in physics and engineering.

Linear Algebra

Linear Algebra and Linear Algebra II, including vector spaces, matrices, eigenvalues, and their role in mathematical modeling.

Physics and Space Science

Teaching experience includes space science, gravity, relativity, quantum mechanics, classical mechanics, and continuum mechanics.

Learning Support

Educational work on mathematics and physics support through the peer-tutor system known as Sugaku-Butsuri Kakekomidera.

Textbook

Textbook

A textbook that carries first-year calculus toward vector analysis and differential equations.

Differential and Integral Calculus and Its Applications

Umpei Miyamoto, Kyoritsu Shuppan, released on Nov. 22, 2022. ISBN 9784320114807.

Publisher page

The book emphasizes not only calculation techniques, but also a view of calculus as a bridge toward vector analysis, differential equations, physics, and engineering.
