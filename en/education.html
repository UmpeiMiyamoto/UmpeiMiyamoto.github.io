Courses and Education

Education

From calculus and linear algebra to relativity, quantum theory, fluid mechanics, and space science. The emphasis is on treating mathematics not as a list of formulas to memorize, but as a language for describing the world.

Teaching

Courses and Teaching Focus

Miyamoto teaches courses that connect foundational mathematics with theoretical physics, mainly at Akita Prefectural University.

Mathematical Foundations

Calculus, linear algebra, and logic, with emphasis on calculation, structure, and the language needed for engineering and physics.

Relativity and Quantum Theory

Gravity, relativity, and quantum mechanics presented as a natural continuation of geometry and linear algebra.

Fluids and Continua

Classical mechanics, continuum mechanics, and fluid mechanics, focusing on how equations describe shapes, flows, and stability.

Space Science and Support

Space science, science education, and mathematics/physics support through the peer-tutor system known as Sugaku-Butsuri Kakekomidera.
