Routes

Main ways to get there

Travel times are approximate and based on the university's official access information. Train and bus schedules may vary by day and season.

Airport

About 50 minutes by car from Akita Airport.

JR

About 45 minutes by JR train from Akita Station to Ugo-Honjo Station.

Local bus

From Ugo-Honjo Station, take a local bus to Kenritsu Daigaku Iriguchi. The ride is about 5 minutes, followed by about 15 minutes on foot.

Campus bus

The Kenritsu Daigaku Line runs from Honjo Ekimae to Kenritsu Daigaku-mae at the campus rotary in about 7 minutes. It does not operate on weekends, national holidays, or during long university vacation periods.

Bus timetables and service patterns can change. Please confirm current information with Ugo Kotsu or other transport operators before departure.
